TMGR Mission Statement

About Us
The purpose of our club is to have FUN with MGs, maintain the marque and enrich the MG owner/enthusiast experience through social collaboration with fellow like-minded members, who are united in encouraging the restoration, preservation, and driving of MGs.

We Strive:

    1. To further the general enjoyment of our hobby and promote awareness of the marque.
    2. To promote, organize, and supervise outings, meetings, shows, events, and Gatherings of the Faithful.
    3. To help our fellow enthusiasts with technical and non-technical challenges with their vehicles.
    4. To build fellowship and new friendships through activities with or without our cars.
    5. To foster working relationships with local MG clubs through collaboration and cross-promotion.
    6. To promote collaboration with all national and international MG organizations.
